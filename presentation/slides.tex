\subsection*{Problem}

Optimal humidity: 35--50\%.

Air gets dry when indoor heating is used.

During winter: $<30\%$.

Respiratory infections, irritated skin and eyes.

\subsection*{Solution}

Humidifier.

User-programmable, energy-efficient, closed-loop control.
